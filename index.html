About

I am a Junior Software Developer, an inquisitive learner who found interest in Cloud and Web development. Working towards becoming a Full Stack Developer by June 2021.

My journey into tech start at Puma Teamwear as a graphic designer and been expose to WordPress. I became curious about its more in-depth functions and how it’s created. For a better understanding, I studied WordPress, then created a website for my home village.

As I understood more, my passion grew and I became eager to learn more. I came across CodeandStuff a coding and networking community for women and non-binary developers and code newbies in Manchester. The community allowed me to deepen my knowledge of HTML, CSS and JavaScript, and expose me to various online schools, such as Codecademy, W3Schools, and SoloLearn, and shortly after I developed my first website and was able to utilize skills like Agile and TDD (Test Driven Development) which I learned while attending CodeandStuff weekly. The community also helped me improve my communication skills, socialise and introduced me to fellow learners and developers in the industry.

I am very flexible and have an aptitude to learn because I understand tech is constantly evolving, and I have taken part in various events to learn about new technologies, including IOT, containers, GIS and autonomous services.

I am resilient, curious and enthusiastic programmer with excellent communication and problem-solving skills, who aspire to make positive impact. I strive to build strong relationships based on honest communication, attention to details and flexible approach. During previous roles, I developed a hardworking, organised and positive attitude, which I’m confident is a great addition to a team. I am motivated to take a step further to my personal development and career progression and looking to join a company where I can put to action my skills and knowledge and face new challenges, as a Junior Software Developer.
